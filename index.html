About the Series

Monday Military Madness is a seven-stage community Zwift race series, with a new battlefield every month. Each round brings a different test, from flatter, faster racing to harder courses where positioning, pacing and timing matter.

The series is designed to be competitive, accessible and fun, giving riders the chance to fight for overall points, GC, sprint honours and K/QOM rewards across the campaign. Whether you are racing for the podium or just turning up for the challenge, every stage adds to the story.

Quick Rules

  • Monday Military Madness is a community event and open to all riders unless otherwise stated.
  • Riders must enter the correct category or pen for each event.
  • Race cards show the official format, laps, distance, elevation, sprint and K/QOM details for each round.
  • Official results and standings are published after each event.
  • Riders are expected to race fairly and in good spirit at all times.
  • The organiser may review and amend results where needed to protect fair competition.
